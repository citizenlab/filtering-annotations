Access to this site has been blocked by an Order issued by the Federal Court of Canada on November 15, 2019 in Court File T-1169-19.

The Plaintiffs in Court File T-1169-19 are Bell Media Inc., Group TVA Inc., and Rogers Media Inc.

You may apply to the Federal Court of Canada to seek a variation of this Order if you are the operator of this site or of another website that is affected by this Order; or an internet service customer affected by this Order, and the Order affects your ability to access or distribute non-infringing content. To seek such a variation, you must serve and file a motion record within thirty (30) days of first being affected by this Order.
