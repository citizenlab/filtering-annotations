Access to the location you have attempted to reach has been disabled pursuant to an Order of the Federal Court (Canada) dated November 15, 2019, in Federal Court File No. T-1169-19. If you are the operator of any of the Target Websites listed below, the operator of another website you believe to be affected by the Order, or an Internet service customer affected by the Order, you may bring a motion in the Federal Court to seek a variation of this Order insofar as it affects your ability to access or distribute non-infringing content. The Target Websites affected by this Order are: the GoldTV.biz service and the GoldTV.ca service. The Order may apply to any domain, subdomain or IP address that has as its sole or predominant purpose to enable or facilitate access to the services of these Target Websites.

 

The Plaintiffs associated with this Order are Bell Media Inc., Groupe TVA Inc. and Rogers Media Inc.
